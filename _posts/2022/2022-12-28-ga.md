## geometric product

Definition of geometric product (understanding with reference to complex number):

$$
\vec{u}\vec{v} = \vec{u} \cdot \vec{v} + \vec{u} \wedge \vec{v} = \|\vec{u} \| \|\vec{v} \|e^{i\theta}
$$
