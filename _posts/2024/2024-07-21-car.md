[Why China is winning the EV war](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rkxMdmipYqM) - **BATTERY**. 40% cost of producing a EV is in battery.
* CATL - NingDe, the largest battery company in the world.
* BYD, the largest EV company.
* (1) government support in EV industry.
  * foreign EV company must use chinese-made batteries to qualify for consumer subsides.
* (2) through the supply chain for the battery components.
  * [Lithium-ion battery](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lithium-ion_battery) - Electrolyte (Lithium), Anode (Graphite), Separator, Cathode (Nickel, Cobalt, Manganses).
  * Supply : control significant percentages of the world's supply of the minerals needed for batteries.
  * After mining : china refines a vast majority of them.
* (3) Technological innovation.
  * LFP (Lithium iron phosphate) : avoid using Nickel & Cobalt.
